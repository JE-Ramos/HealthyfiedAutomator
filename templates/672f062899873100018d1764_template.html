Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp, Olive Oil
  • 300 g, Beef Samgyupsal
  • 50 gram, Onions Raw (White)
  • 1 medium, Carrots
  • 1 tbsp, Soy sauce
  • 1 medium, Red bell pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients: thinly slice the Amir's Beef Striploin Steak, dice the onions, julienne the carrots, and thinly slice the red bell pepper.
  2. In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp of olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced onions and julienne carrots to the pan and saut√© until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the thinly sliced beef to the pan and cook until it's no longer pink, about 8 minutes.
  5. Stir in 1 tbsp of soy sauce and continue cooking for another 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Add the sliced red bell pepper to the pan, cooking for an additional 2 minutes or until everything is heated through and the peppers are slightly tender.
  7. Serve your Healthyfied Beef Samgyupsal hot, garnished with your choice of herbs for an extra touch of flavor.
